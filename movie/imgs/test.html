Synopsis

A 30-minute documentary revealing the high-stakes legislative battle that erupted when California attempted to regulate frontier AI systems.

SB-1047 documents the unprecedented political battle that erupted when California introduced the first major AI safety bill in the United States. At its center stands Governor Gavin Newsom, caught between two powerful coalitions fighting for control over humanity's most transformative technology.

On one side: State Senator Scott Wiener backed by AI safety researchers, Hollywood celebrities like Mark Ruffalo, and even Elon Musk. On the other: tech giants led by Mark Zuckerberg, venture capital powerhouse Marc Andreessen, and political heavyweight Nancy Pelosi.

Through exclusive access to key players, this documentary reveals the high-stakes lobbying war that unfolded both online and within Sacramento's Capitol corridors. Viewers will witness firsthand how powerful interests clash in determining how we govern AI before potentially catastrophic risks become reality.

With AI advancing at an unprecedented pace, SB-1047 offers a rare window into the power struggles that will define how we manage this transformative technology – and ultimately, our collective future.

Michaël Trazzi, Director

Michaël Trazzi is the founder of The Inside View, Inc., a production company dedicated to exploring AI developments through in-depth interviews with leading researchers and policymakers. Having conducted over 40 interviews about artificial intelligence, his channel has built an engaged audience of over 20,000 followers across YouTube and Twitter.

For SB-1047, he embarked on an ambitious two-month journey across the United States, conducting over 20 interviews with politicians, activists, and researchers to document the intense battle surrounding what would become the most controversial AI regulation proposal to date.
