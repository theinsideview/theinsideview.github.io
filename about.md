**DeepMind Hunger Strike**

In September 2025, Michaël started the Google DeepMind Hunger Strike, which was covered in mainstream news outlets like <a href="https://www.telegraph.co.uk/business/2025/09/20/anti-ai-doom-prophets-launch-hunger-strike-outside-google/">The Telegraph</a>, <a href="https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/778773/the-hunger-strike-to-end-ai-anthropic-google-deepmind-agi">The Verge</a>, <a href="https://news.sky.com/video/hunger-strikers-want-end-to-superhuman-ai-13434270">Sky News</a>, and <a href="https://www.lemonde.fr/pixels/article/2025/09/18/nous-allons-perdre-le-controle-comment-les-catastrophistes-de-l-ia-haussent-le-ton_6641620_4408996.html">Le Monde</a>, and received <a href="https://x.com/DSheremet_/status/1967894578876272698?s=20">internal support</a> from DeepMind employees, and I believe eventually led to Demis Hassabis giving a <a href="https://x.com/MichaelTrazzi/status/2013787511189876959?s=20">public answer</a> on whether he would pause frontier AI development if everyone else also paused.
